About Our Studio

LC Architects is an internationally recognized architecture firm known for our innovative approach to design and commitment to sustainable solutions.

Redefining Architectural Excellence Since 2005

Founded by Lucas Chen, LC Architects has grown from a small boutique studio to an award-winning international practice with projects spanning four continents. Our work is characterized by a thoughtful response to context, innovative use of materials, and a commitment to environmental stewardship.

We believe architecture should tell a story - one that reflects the aspirations of our clients while respecting the cultural and environmental context of each project. Our process is collaborative, bringing together clients, consultants, and craftsmen to create buildings that inspire and endure.

With offices in New York, London, and Tokyo, our diverse team of architects and designers brings a global perspective to every project, from private residences to cultural institutions and urban masterplans.
